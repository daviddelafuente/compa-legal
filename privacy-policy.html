How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Habit Tracking: Store your selected habits and daily completion status
  • Character System: Calculate your companion's mood based on habit consistency
  • Journal Features: Save and organize your daily journal entries
  • Progress Analytics: Show your habit completion trends and statistics
  • Weather Display: Show current weather conditions (location used temporarily)

Personalization

  • Display your name throughout the app
  • Customize age-appropriate features based on your birthday
  • Sync your data across all your Apple devices

Data Storage & Security

‚òÅÔ∏èApple iCloud Integration

All your personal data is stored using Apple's CloudKit technology, which means:

  • Your data stays in your iCloud account - we never have access to it
  • End-to-end encryption protects your information in transit and at rest
  • Apple's security standards protect your data with industry-leading security
  • You control your data - you can delete it anytime from your iCloud settings

Local Device Storage

When you use Compa without signing in, your data is stored locally on your device using Core Data, Apple's secure local database system. This data is protected by your device's security features including device passcode and biometric authentication.

Third-Party Services

Weather Information

We use OpenWeatherMap to provide current weather conditions. When you allow location access:

  • Your location is sent directly to OpenWeatherMap to get weather data
  • We don't store your location - it's only used temporarily for weather requests
  • You can disable location access anytime in iOS Settings
  • Weather display is optional and doesn't affect core app functionality

Apple Sign-In

When you sign in with Apple:

  • Apple provides us with your name and email (or a private email relay)
  • This information is stored in your iCloud account, not on our servers
  • Apple's privacy protections apply to this authentication process

Your Privacy Rights

Access & Control

  • View your data: All your data is visible within the Compa app
  • Edit your information: Update your profile anytime in Settings
  • Delete your data: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings or delete from iCloud
  • Export your data: Contact us for data export assistance

Location & Permissions

  • Location access: Only when you explicitly allow it for weather features
  • Revoke permissions: Change anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security
  • No tracking: We don't track your location or movements

Children's Privacy

Compa is suitable for users of all ages. For users under 13:

  • We follow the same privacy-first approach
  • No additional data collection occurs
  • Parents can review and manage data through iCloud Family settings
  • We recommend parental guidance when setting up accounts for children

International Users

Compa is available worldwide. Your data storage location depends on your Apple ID country setting:

  • iCloud data is stored in Apple's data centers in your region
  • Apple complies with local data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Weather data requests are processed by OpenWeatherMap's global service

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date below
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes
  • You can always review the current policy at this URL
